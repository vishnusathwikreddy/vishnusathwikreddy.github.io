你好，我是Sathwik Reddy。

I'm an undergraduate researcher at IIIT Hyderabad, working with the Precog research group under Dr. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru (PK).

I'm currently investigating privacy in personalized AI systems and reasoning in continuous latent spaces. If you're interested in similar (or related) problems, I'm happy to connect!

In general, I like working on problems with real-world impact. I am particularly interested in AI Safety, with focus on making AI systems more robust, interpretable, and trustworthy for reliable deployment. I'm fortunate to collaborate with Prof. Amartya Sanyal (University of Copenhagen) and Prof. Jisun An (Indiana University). Previously, I worked with Prof. Konjengbam Anand and Rachit Verma on Legal NLP.

You can find me on GitHub, Twitter, or reach me via email.
