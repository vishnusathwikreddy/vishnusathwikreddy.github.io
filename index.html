你好，我是Sathwik Reddy。

I'm an undergraduate researcher in Computer Science at IIIT Hyderabad. I'm part of the Precog research group, mentored by Dr. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru (PK).
Previously, I worked at the EMA Lab at IIT Dharwad with Dr. Konjengbam Anand and Rachit Verma, focusing on Legal NLP, and attended the IASNLP 2024 summer school. You can find me on GitHub and Twitter, or reach me via email.

Research interests

  • Reasoning in Continuous Latent Spaces: exploring reasoning within continuous, rather than discrete, latent representations.
  • Context-Aware AI: using richer context to make models more reliable.
  • Mathematical Problem-Solving: improving models on structured, symbolic tasks.
